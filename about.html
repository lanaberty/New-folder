About

The aim of this priject is to design a vector illustration for an A4 poster series for *MaltaDoors*, a visual blog that documents the colourful and quirky facades and doorways on the Maltese Islands. For this project I will be conducting visual research, producing a digital sketch and designing the illustration using Adobe Illustrator.

Visual Research

I photographically explored the quirky streets of Maltese towns and villages and collected images that will inspire my artwork. This is a collection of photos of multiple traditonal Maltese doors that I took with my camera in the streets of Birgu.

Sketch

I choose one door from the photographs which I wanted to convert into a vector illustration and analysed the design elements of the facade. I made sure to simplify and stylise all the details to focus on the most essential visual elements of the Doors.

Vector Illustration

Using Adobe Illustrator, I produced a stylised vector illustration of the selected door. This is a simple minimanalist design coloured with a bright modern colour scheme inspired by my visual research.
